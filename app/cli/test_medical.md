# Medical Terms

## Cardiovascular System

**Hypertension**: A condition characterized by abnormally high blood pressure. Also known as *high blood pressure*.

### Common Medications

- **ACE inhibitors**: Drugs that lower blood pressure by blocking angiotensin-converting enzyme
- **Beta blockers**: Medications that reduce heart rate and cardiac output
- **Diuretics**: Water pills that help remove excess fluid

## Respiratory System

`Pneumonia` is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs.

```
Common symptoms include:
- Cough with phlegm
- Fever and chills
- Shortness of breath
```

### Diagnostic Tests

1. Chest X-ray
2. Blood tests
3. Pulse oximetry

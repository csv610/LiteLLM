# Article Review

This module reviews a medical article and produces structured editing or critique output.

## Files

- `article_review.py`: review logic.
- `article_review_cli.py`: CLI interface.
- `article_review_models.py`: schemas.
- `article_review_prompts.py`: prompts.

## Limitations

- It reviews article text, not the truth of the underlying medical claims.
- Suggested edits should be reviewed before publication or submission.

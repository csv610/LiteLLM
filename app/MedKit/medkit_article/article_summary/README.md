# Article Summary

This module summarizes a medical article into a shorter structured or narrative output.

## Files

- `article_summary.py`: summary logic.
- `article_summary_cli.py`: CLI interface.
- `article_summary_models.py`: schemas.
- `article_summary_prompts.py`: prompts.

## Limitations

- Summaries may omit nuance, caveats, or study limitations.
- The result should not replace direct reading of the source article.

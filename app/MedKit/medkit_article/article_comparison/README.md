# Article Comparison

This module compares two medical articles and produces a structured side-by-side summary.

## Files

- `article_comparison.py`: comparison logic.
- `article_comparison_cli.py`: CLI interface.
- `article_comparison_models.py`: schemas.
- `article_comparison_prompts.py`: prompts.

## Limitations

- Comparison quality depends on the quality of the source files and model output.
- The result is a summary aid, not a formal critical appraisal.

# Medical Legal Rights

This package generates educational material about patient rights and healthcare legal topics.

## Files

- `legal_rights.py`: generation logic.
- `legal_rights_cli.py`: CLI interface.
- `legal_rights_models.py`: schemas.
- `legal_rights_prompts.py`: prompts.

## Why It Matters

Patient-rights material is often difficult to summarize in plain language. This module provides a structured generator for that purpose.

## Limitations

- Legal information is jurisdiction-specific and time-sensitive.
- The output is educational material, not legal advice.
